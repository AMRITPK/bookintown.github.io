Refund Policies


Yet to receive my refund on credit/debit card
It's been a week and the refund has still not been credited in my credit/debit card account. Why is it taking so long? A reversal request for the captured amount is sent to the bank by the end of the day and the reversal period of the amount depends on each bank's policy which can be up to 5-7 working days from the date of transacting and 10 working days in case of a nationalized bank. In case you have not received the amount, please contact us on +91 9746696122 or write to us at customerservice@bookintown.com.

Refund Amount not received

My card has been charged, by when will I receive the refund? As per our company policy, we send a reversal request to the bank by the end of the day. Depending on the card issuing bank, it takes up to 5-7 working days and a maximum of 10 working days in case of a nationalized bank for the reversal to take effect.

If it has been longer than 10 days, please feel free to contact us on customerservice@bookintown.com or call us on +91 9746696122 and we will take this up on priority with the bank to ensure the refund is done immediately.

My transaction Failed, what do I do now?

My account has been debited for an unsuccessful transaction attempt. How can I get a refund of my money?
BookInTown is really sorry for the trouble caused.
In case of an unsuccessful/failed attempt of booking the amount charged is refunded back within 4 hours from us and should reflect back in your account within a max of 5-7 working days, in case it’s a nationalized bank it would take a maximum of 10 working days You can also get in touch with us at customerservice@bookintown.com, or contact us at +91 9746696122 with the contact details entered while transacting.

Booked my tickets was charged but did not get a confirmation mail/sms?

This may be due to a temporary network fluctuation. BookInTown is sorry to know about this.

Ideally, if you have been charged for an unsuccessful transaction attempt the amount should reflect back in your account/ wallet within a max of 5-7 working days, incase it’s a nationalized bank it would take a maximum of 10 working days .
